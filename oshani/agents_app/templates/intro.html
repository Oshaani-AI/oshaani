FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything sales and engineering teams ask before saying yes.

How fast can I launch a real agent?
Most teams ship a working agent in under 15 minutes. Pick a model, attach a knowledge base, optionally add tools or MCP, then test in the built-in chat or call it via API.
Which models are supported?
Amazon Bedrock (Claude, Llama, Titan, Mistral and more) and self-hosted Ollama models. You can switch the model on any agent without rewriting prompts.
How is my data handled and secured?
Your knowledge bases are isolated per workspace, traffic is encrypted in transit, API keys are scoped per agent, and you control sharing and access. Enterprise plans add SSO and audit logs.
Can I use Oshaani commercially?
The open source license permits non-commercial use only. Commercial use requires a separate license from the project maintainers.
Do you support custom tools and MCP?
Yes. Define tools as JSON, import OpenAPI specs, or connect any MCP server—agents can act on real systems with full audit trails.
Is it free to use?
Yes, for non-commercial purposes. Clone the repository, self-host, and use the full platform without subscription or payment setup.
